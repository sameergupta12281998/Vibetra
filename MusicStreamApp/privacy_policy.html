Privacy Policy for Vibetra

Effective date: April 6, 2026

Vibetra ("we", "our", or "us") built the Vibetra app as a free application. This page informs users about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when using our app.

Information We Do Not Collect

Vibetra does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, location, or contacts. We do not require account registration.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect information about you. Links to their privacy policies are provided below:

  • Google AdMob – Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads.
  • Audius – Music streaming platform. Audio streams are fetched via the public Audius API in accordance with provider terms.
  • Jamendo – Creative Commons music catalog. Tracks are fetched via the public Jamendo API in accordance with provider terms.
  • YouTube Data API – Used for music search metadata only. No audio is streamed from YouTube.
  • YouTube API Services Terms – Usage of YouTube API data follows YouTube API Services policies.

Copyright and Licensing

Music, artwork, and metadata displayed in Vibetra are provided by third-party services and remain the property of their respective owners and/or licensors. Vibetra does not claim ownership of third-party content.

Vibetra does not provide functionality to download, rip, or extract copyrighted media from third-party platforms. Playback is limited to direct stream URLs made available by content providers.

Trademark and Affiliation Disclaimer

Vibetra is an independent application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Google, YouTube, Spotify, Audius, or Jamendo. All third-party names and trademarks are the property of their respective owners.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to show personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings under Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

Internet Access

The app requires an internet connection to stream music and display ads. No usage data or listening history is sent to our servers — all history and favorites are stored locally on your device only.

Children's Privacy

Vibetra is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
